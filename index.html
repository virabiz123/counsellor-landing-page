About

"I believe healing happens in a space of warmth, curiosity, and no judgment."

I'm a licensed therapist with over 12 years of experience helping people move through anxiety, depression, relationship struggles, grief, and major life changes. My approach draws from evidence-based modalities — but more importantly, from deeply listening to what each person truly needs.

I offer both in-person sessions in Austin, TX and secure online therapy for clients across Texas.
